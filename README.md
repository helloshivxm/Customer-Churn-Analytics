# Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

# Project Overview:
Customer churn is one of the most critical challenges for subscription-based businesses.This project analyzes customer behavior, identifies churn drivers, quantifies revenue at risk, and provides actionable retention strategies through SQL, Excel, and Power BI.

# The objective was to help management understand:

Why customers are churning
Which customer segments are at highest risk
How much revenue is at risk
What actions can reduce churn

# Business Problem:

The telecom company was experiencing customer attrition, leading to revenue leakage and reduced customer lifetime value.

# Management required a data-driven solution to:

Identify high-risk customers
Understand churn patterns
Quantify business impact
Improve retention efforts

# 🛠 Tools & Technologies
SQL, Excel, Power BI, DAX

# Key Findings

1. New Customers Are Most Likely To Churn
Customers with tenure between 0–12 months exhibit the highest churn rate.

Business Impact: 
Lower customer lifetime value
Higher acquisition costs
Reduced retention

2. Contract Type Is The Strongest Churn Driver

88.55% of churned customers are on Month-to-Month contracts.

Business Impact

Low commitment
Higher switching behavior
Revenue instability

3. Revenue Exposure Is Significant

Approximately ₹139K revenue is currently associated with churned customers.

Business Impact

Revenue leakage
Reduced profitability
Lower growth potential

4. Service Adoption Influences Retention

Customers without Online Security and Tech Support services show significantly higher churn rates.

5. Electronic Check Customers Have Higher Churn

Payment behavior appears strongly associated with customer retention.

# Business Recommendations

Immediate Actions
Launch retention campaigns for Month-to-Month customers
Improve onboarding during first 12 months
Promote Online Security and Tech Support adoption
Encourage Auto-Pay migration
Strategic Actions
Improve customer engagement
Introduce contract upgrade incentives
Monitor high-risk customer segments proactively
